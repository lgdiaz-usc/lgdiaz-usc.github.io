Problem Statement: Boredom

In a perfect world, there would be no boredom. Currently, we are plagued by it almost every day in some form or fashion. This boredom is wasting our time that we can be productive during. To solve this, an app should be created to help fill those little, or big, times of boredom with productive and entertaining activities.
